Support

Legal help is one part.
Wellbeing matters too.

Alongside our legal services, we point fathers toward trusted external resources — for mental health, community, financial guidance, and reliable health information.

A father who is supported — mentally, financially, socially — shows up better in every room that matters. These organisations have helped many of our clients through difficult times. We encourage you to explore them alongside your legal matter.

MensLine Australia

MensLine Australia

Confidential telephone and online counselling, referral, and support for men. Available 24/7 on 1300 78 99 78.

mensline.org.au →

Beyond Blue

Beyond Blue

Mental health support, information, and resources for anxiety, depression, and crisis support. Helpline: 1300 22 4636.

beyondblue.org.au →

Men's Shed

Men's Shed

Community sheds across Australia where men connect, share skills, and support each other. Over 1,000 sheds nationwide.

mensshed.org →

Healthdirect

Healthdirect

Free Australian government health advice and information — available online or by phone at 1800 022 222, 24 hours a day.

healthdirect.gov.au →

Sonium Finance

Sonium Finance Group

Financial guidance and assistance to help you plan and rebuild after separation — budgeting, lending, and financial strategy.

soniumfinance.com.au →
